2026-07-14 — First rehearsal using the silent controller

First rehearsal using the new silent controller.

The immediate response feels much more natural and fluid than the previous version with click switches.

Playing observations :

I almost always latch North and ping South.

This makes sense because:
North = Attract
Attracting the pendulum towards the centre takes time.
The magnetic field usually needs to remain on until the pendulum has been pulled into position.
A latched field is therefore the most natural gesture.
South = Repel
Repel is usually used as a short impulse.
A brief pulse can either:
add momentum immediately after the pendulum passes the coil, or
bounce it away from the centre.
A ping feels like the natural way to play this gesture.

Leaving South latched generally reduces the pendulum's momentum, although it creates some interesting behaviours that I will occasionally use as part of the performance.

Controller observations
The current interaction model suits these playing techniques very well.
At the moment I do not feel a need to change the controller behaviour.

The distinction between:
short press = ping
long press = latch

matches the physical behaviour of the pendulum remarkably well.

Future experiments:
Continue rehearsing before making firmware changes.
Experiment with different latch times after more playing experience.
